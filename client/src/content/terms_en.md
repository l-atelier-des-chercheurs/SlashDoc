## 2. Service Purpose and Philosophy

The /doc (SlashDoc) platform aims to:

- document research, creation and production processes in design;
- promote the sharing of situated knowledge, methods, tools and resources;
- contribute to an ecosystem of open, reproducible and appropriable practices by local actors.

/doc (SlashDoc) is part of a commons logic, considering knowledge as a collective resource that must be able to circulate, be enriched, criticized and transformed, while respecting people, contexts and territories of origin.
